Delete Your Glimpse Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request deletion of your Glimpse account and all associated data by following the steps below.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

How to Delete Your Account

Step 1 Send an email to bhaveshjavvaji@gmail.com with the subject line: "Delete My Glimpse Account"
Step 2 Include the email address associated with your Google account used to sign in to Glimpse.
Step 3 We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm deletion via email.

What Data Gets Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your Google account profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Saved articles
  • Quiz scores and streaks
  • Topic preferences and interests
  • All other personal data associated with your account

What Data May Be Retained

Some anonymized, non-personal analytics data may be retained for up to 30 days for technical and legal reasons, after which it will also be permanently deleted.
