Glimpse — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Glimpse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Glimpse.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including articles read, quiz scores, and preferences.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, and app version for analytics and debugging.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Glimpse app experience
  • To personalize your news feed based on your interests
  • To track your quiz progress and streaks
  • To send push notifications (only if you opt in)
  • To analyze app performance and fix bugs

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which complies with industry-standard security practices. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Glimpse uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database
  • Google Sign-In — user authentication
  • Google Analytics — app usage analytics

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Glimpse is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
